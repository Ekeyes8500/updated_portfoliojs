About

My name is Eric Keyes, and I am an aspiring Web Developer. Originally from Bensalem, my family moved out to the Harrisburg area when I was a child. I (thankfully!) was able to escape back into Wawa territory recently. There was always an underlying interest in code throughout my formative years. Whether it was patching bits of lua together that I googled for a script that I "made" for Garry's Mod, or trying to wrap my head around what made Morrowind tick, I was always picking apart whatever computer game code I could get my hands on.

As I grew up, my interest in technology stuck with, but without resources to continue college, I found myself landing a job in cellphone sales. I found quite a bit of success in the sales world, eventually attaining a store as General Manager. Together with my team, we fostered an incredible working environment and we were very successful in reaching our predetermined goals. However, eventually our company (Best Buy) decided that, as a whole, they'd be better off without the Mobile Stores and ended up closing every single one.

At first, losing such a well paying, (seemingly) secure job felt like such a blow. However, it wasn't long before I signed up for the UPENN coding bootcamp program, and I quickly realized that I had a legitimate passion for development. Not only was I completely enthralled by creating things with javascript, I found my unique people skills incredibly useful in team development situations. So with my unique skillset and background, I hope to be the perfect fit for the industry.
